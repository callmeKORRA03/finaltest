Our Goals And Vision

VISION
To be a link between local sellers and buyers, bridging the gap and covering the exigencies of delivery in rural and urban areas.


MISSION
To provide efficient yet affordable and environmentally friendly deliveries. To eradicate the scamming culture in e-commerce, inflicting a severe repercussion on the Nigerian market. To build a strong distribution network that will aid small and medium-sized businesses to have a wide margin market.

DOORYD IS NOT A LOGISTICS COMPANY, Dooryd is an online Company in the Business Business of Connecting Logistic Riders, Vendors and Customers. An online platform where people can buy goods without the fear of being scammed, delivery can be covered as quickly as possible. Dooryd would venture into the E-hailing cab business helping People move from one place to the other locally and Nationally.
